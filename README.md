# JBH Monolithic  [Java & Angular]

Just Be Honest [Java/SpringBoot version] is the backend component of a self-managed personal finance tracking software. The idea of this personal project is to offer me a comprehensive overview of my financial landscape by allowing manual input and management of financial account data, transfers, and expenses. With a focus on user-driven control, 'Just Be Honest' empowers me to take charge of my financial well-being.
